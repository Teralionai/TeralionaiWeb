Owl AI PM — How We Build Software

In early 2024, we took on an AI conditional order system for a major brokerage. Three engineers, one tester, one PM — three months to deliver.

By week six, the project had spiraled out of control.

Requirements were scattered across Slack, Notion, and Email. Engineers were waiting on each other's APIs with no one knowing who was blocked. The PM spent two hours daily on progress reports that were outdated by the time they were finished. Test cases were written, but code hadn't started.

We realized: the problem wasn't the people, it was the process. So we paused the project for a week, laid out every pain point, and asked ourselves one question —

"If the PM itself were AI, how would it manage this project?"

Owl is the answer to that question.

Owl platform in action — AI coordinates Workers to fix a 500 error, from discovery to resolution in under 5 minutes

Five Advantages of Building with Owl

01

Just Say It, and Work Begins

No PRDs, no Sprint Planning. Describe your requirements in natural language, and Owl generates a structured SPEC. Iterate through discussion until you're satisfied, then development begins. What you save isn't just documentation time — it's the "requirements misalignment" that kills projects.

02

Tests Before Code

Owl defines test cases with the Tester role before development starts. TDD isn't an ideal — it's the default workflow. Every line of code has quality assurance from birth. Post-launch rework dropped by 70% in our measurements.

03

Six Roles, One Owl Orchestrating

Engineer, Tester, Marketing, RD, CICD — each role does its part while Owl auto-assigns tasks, monitors status, and relays handoff messages. No one needs to ask "what's the current progress?" because Owl's Dashboard is always up to date.

04

Instant Blocker Alerts, No Daily Standups

In traditional projects, a blocked engineer waits until the next standup to be noticed. With Owl, Worker status pushes in real time, and blockers auto-escalate to PM level. Time from discovery to resolution: from 24 hours down to minutes.

05

From MVP to Production, Auto-iterated

Owl splits every project into MVP, Phase 1, Phase 2... each phase focused on a single theme. After acceptance, it automatically moves to the next phase. No more "never-ending v1" — our clients see their first runnable version within two weeks on average.
